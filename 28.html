About COVID-19

COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the coronavirus. It spreads through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

Common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Body aches and fatigue

How to Protect Yourself

  • ✅ Wear a mask in crowded places.
  • ✅ Wash your hands frequently with soap and water.
  • ✅ Maintain physical distance of at least 6 feet.
  • ✅ Get vaccinated and stay updated with booster shots.
  • ✅ Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
